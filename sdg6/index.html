Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

Why SDG 6 matters

Clean water and sanitation are essential for health, dignity, and sustainable development. This goal aims to provide safe drinking water, end open defecation, and protect water ecosystems.

Safe Drinking Water

Everyone should have access to safe, affordable drinking water by 2030.

Sanitation Facilities

End open defecation and provide adequate sanitation for all communities.

Water Quality

Reduce pollution, eliminate dumping, and improve water quality worldwide.

Water Efficiency

Increase water-use efficiency and ensure sustainable withdrawals.

Take action

Conserve water, support clean water initiatives, and advocate for sanitation access.
