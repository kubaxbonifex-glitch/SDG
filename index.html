Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promoting sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

Why SDG 8 matters

Decent work and economic growth help reduce poverty, improve living standards, and build resilient communities. This goal focuses on safe jobs, equal pay, youth employment, and sustainable business.

Decent Employment

Every person deserves fair wages, safe working conditions, and social protection.

Youth Opportunities

Young people need access to training, internships, and entrepreneurship support.

Economic Growth

Sustainable growth stimulates innovation, boosts productivity, and creates better livelihoods.

Take action

Support local businesses, learn about workplace rights, and encourage fair hiring practices.
